Welcome to Canela

Canela, meaning Cinnamon (or shin) in Portuguese, is a town located in the Serra Ga√∫cha of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Both Canela and neighboring Gramado are important tourist locations and they both draw many visitors each year. Ecotourism is very popular in the area and there are many opportunities for hiking, rock climbing, horseback riding and river rafting.

The best time to visit Canela

The busiest month for tourism in Canela, Brazil is January, followed by November and May. Prices for hotels and flights will be most expensive during these months, though you can save if you purchase well in advance. Tourists are unlikely to visit Canela in September. Those willing to visit at these times will likely find it the least expensive month.
